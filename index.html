about me

I'm a computer science student at UC Irvine, and will be graduating in Winter of 2023.

My interest in computer science began during the last two years of high school. At the time, I had discovered web development and began to learn HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. After graduating from high school, I knew I wanted to pursue a bachelors in a tech related field. However, I was unsure if computer science was the right path for me. I completed my lower-division course work at a local community college and after some time decided to transfer to UC Irvine and pursure a bachelors.

During my time at UC Irvine, I have enjoyed coursework in systems programming (e.g., computer architecture, operating systems, and compilers). During my free time outside of school, I'm also interested in full-stack development. At the moment, I am learning how to build full-stack applications using Java.
